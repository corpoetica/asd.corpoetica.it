How to pay with Ethereum

1. Get some Ethereum

Primalbase accepts payments only in Ethereum (ETH), so you need to get some first. If you already have it, feel free to skip to step 2. You can buy Ethereum for your preferred fiat currency (USD, EUR, GBP etc.) on any digital currency exchange such as Coinbase, Changelly, Kraken, Poloniex and many others. The process is usually straightforward, but it might take a few days to verify your identity there.

2. Transfer Ethereum to your private wallet

Now you need to transfer your ETH from digital currency exchange (see step 1) to your private wallet such as MetaMask, MyEtherWallet or Ledger. Avoid paying for your Primalbase bookings directly from exchanges as we won't be able to refund your payment in refund cases.

3. Pay for your Primalbase booking

To pay for your booking, you’ll need to transfer Ethereum from your private wallet (see step 2) to our unique ETH wallet which is shown on Payment details page.

What are Primalbase Tokens?

Primalbase Tokens (PBT) are our own digital asset. It gives you a permanent membership to our workspaces. Community members who own Primalbase Tokens can book a desk (1 PBT) or an entire office (4 PBT) at any Primalbase location worldwide for free.
